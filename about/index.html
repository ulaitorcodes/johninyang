A BRIEF POFILE OF ELDER PROF. JOHN OFEM INYANG

September, 2020
by John Inyang
SHARE POST TWEET POST SHARE POST

Elder Professor John Ofem Inyang was born on the 9th of December, 1968 into a Christian family of HRM Late Chief Ofem Inyang Former Clan Head of Kaboli ward in Idomi, Yakurr Local Government Area of Cross River. He started his Primary Education at Presbyterian Primary School, Idomi and completed at Army Children School Yaba, Lagos State in 1979 and immediately enrolled into Command Secondary School Ipaja, Lagos State. Due to the Military transfer of his father from Lagos to Rivers State. Prof john Inyang returned to the village and completed his secondary education at the Community Secondary School, Idomi in 1984 and obtained WASSCE. Between 1985 and 1986, he was at the Federal School of Arts and Science, Ogoja for his HSC which he never completed as he got admission the following year to study Philosophy at the University Of Calabar, Calabar in 1987. With hard work and commitment, Prof.Inyang successfully graduated from the Department of Philosophy with a second class upper degree in 1991 and did the mandatory National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) programme with the University of Ilorin, Kwara state in 1992. During the service year, he had his first teaching experience as he taught a compulsory Course, ‘Philosophy and Logic’’ to all fresh students in the University of Ilorin. After the NYSC Service year, Prof. Inyang returned and got employed with the Post Primary Schools Management Board (PPSMB), Calabar, Cross River State as a Secondary School teacher in 1992. He served the state in this capacity for five years and 1993, he got admission at the University of Calabar to do a master’s degree programme in public administration (MPA) and completed the programme in 199. In 1998 he was employed by the University of Calabar as a graduate assistant, teaching several courses in the department of philosophy. With the award of study fellowships, Prof John Inyang Obtained another masters degree in 2002 and a P.h.D in 2007 both in philosophy from the University Of Calabar. As an academic, he has taught numerous courses in philosophy at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels for over 17 years, researched and published over 25 articles and a book chapter in both foreign and local journals. He has also published over 25 articles and book chapters in both foreign and local journals. He has also established three philosophy textbooks between 2015 and 201. He is currently a professor of philosophy with bias in Epistemology and Logic. While in the University, prof John Inyang served as a Sub-Dean SSS of the faculty of Arts, Chairman of the Post Graduate result and Thesis Vetting Committee, Former member of the faculty of Arts Curriculum review committee and several other responsibilities to his credit. He has been an academic facilitator with the national open university between 2006 and 2015 and did a one-year sabbatical leave with the University of Portharcourt in 2013. Politically, he is a registered PDP member and contested the House of Assembly primaries twice in 2007 and 2014. He has served the party in various capacities at the state, Local Government and wards levels. Prof John Inyang in 2015 precisely 27th November, was appointed and sworn in as the Hon. Commissioner for Lands And Urban Development by his Excellency Sen.Prof Ben Ayade. Professor Inyang is married and blessed with four lovely children. .
